Compliance Calendar
Federal and Texas HR compliance deadlines by month. Required workplace poster obligations, government reporting, and the ability to track org-specific filing dates in one place.
ℹThis calendar reflects recurring federal and Texas HR compliance obligations. Deadlines may shift when they fall on weekends or federal holidays — verify with counsel for final dates. Some deadlines depend on employer size, benefits plan type, or other org-specific factors. Complete Tool 01 for applicability resolution.
January
Jan 1
New calendar year — audit I-9s for expiring documents
Jan 31
W-2s distributed to employees
Jan 31
Form 941 Q4 filing (or Jan 31 deposit deadline if monthly depositor)
Jan 31
FUTA Form 940 annual filing due
February
Feb 1 — OSHA
OSHA 300A Annual Summary — begin mandatory posting in workplace. Must remain posted through April 30.
Feb 15
Q4 payroll tax deposits due (semi-weekly depositors — varies by payroll date)
Feb 28
Paper W-2 / W-3 transmittal to SSA (if not electronic)
Feb 28
ACA Forms 1094-C / 1095-C paper filing (ALEs)
March
Mar 1
ACA Form 1095-C furnished to employees (ALEs)
Mar 31
Electronic W-2 / W-3 transmittal to SSA
Mar 31
Electronic ACA Forms 1094-C / 1095-C filing
April
Apr 30 — OSHA
OSHA 300A Annual Summary — last day of mandatory posting. Remove after April 30. (Electronic submission to OSHA due March 2 for covered establishments — verify if applicable.)
Apr 30
Form 941 Q1 filing deadline (Q1 = January–March)
Apr 30
Q1 FUTA deposit due (if FUTA liability exceeded $500 in Q1)
Apr 30
Review workplace posters — update any that have changed this year
May
May (ongoing)
Annual benefits open enrollment planning begins for summer/fall
May 31
Texas: Workers compensation coverage renewal check (if applicable)
June
Jun 30
Mid-year payroll audit — verify withholding accuracy
Jun 30
Review handbook for any policy updates needed
July
Jul 31
Form 941 Q2 filing deadline (Q2 = April–June)
Jul 31
Q2 FUTA deposit due (if cumulative FUTA liability exceeded $500)
Jul 31
PCORI fee due (Form 720) — self-insured health plans and certain HRAs
Spring (variable)
EEO-1 Component 1 filing — EEOC opens the collection window annually, typically April through May. Verify the current year deadline at eeoc.gov. Applies to private employers with 100+ employees and federal contractors with 50+ employees and $50K+ in contracts.
August
Aug (variable)
Open enrollment window — distribute SBCs and updated plan materials. Begin enrollment communication checklist (see Tool 08).
Aug 31
Review ACA measurement periods and FTE count for ALE determination if headcount is near 50
September
Jul 31 / Oct 15
Form 5500 for calendar-year plans: original deadline July 31. Extension via Form 5558 (filed by July 31) extends to October 15. Form 5500-SF available for small plans under 100 participants.
Sep 30
CHIP Notice — annual notice to employees about CHIP premium assistance programs. Model notice available from DOL. Required for all employers offering group health plans in states with CHIP programs (including Texas).
October
Oct 14
Medicare Part D creditable coverage notices due to Medicare-eligible employees
Oct 31
Form 941 Q3 filing deadline
November
Nov (ongoing)
Open enrollment closes — confirm all elections are processed
Nov 30
Plan year-end prep: benefits elections finalized, carrier changes implemented
December
Dec 31
Year-end payroll close — final payroll of year processed correctly
Dec 31
Review I-9s for employees whose documents expire in Q1 next year
Dec 31
PTO accrual reconciliation and carryover processing per policy
