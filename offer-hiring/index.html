Rating Rubric — Applies to Every Competency
1

2

⚖ EEO Advisory: Structured, competency-based scoring reduces — but does not eliminate — the risk of inconsistent or discriminatory hiring practices. Scores must be applied consistently across all candidates. Decisions must be based on documented, job-related criteria. Do not allow subjective impressions, referral sources, or cultural assumptions to override the structured process. If a hire decision is challenged, this scorecard and the supporting notes are your primary evidentiary record.
